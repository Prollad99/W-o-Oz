Wizard of Oz Slots Support Guide

Finding Your ZID and Device ID

To locate your ZID and Device ID, access the game settings and select the 'Help' or 'Support' option. The information is typically found under the 'About' section or a similar menu.

Pre-Contact Preparation

Before reaching out to customer support, ensure your game and device software are up to date. Gather relevant information such as your ZID, device ID, and details of your issue to streamline the support process.

Contacting Customer Support

To contact customer support, use the in-game 'Help' option or visit the support website. Provide comprehensive details about your problem for quicker resolution.

Player ID and PIN

Your Player ID and PIN can be found in the game's settings, usually under the 'Account' or 'Profile' sections. This information is crucial for account verification and recovery.

Managing Notifications

For iOS, Android, and Amazon Fire devices, notification settings can be managed through the respective device's settings menu. This includes toggling notifications on or off and customizing alert preferences.

Social Network UID

Your Social Network UID is found in the settings of your connected social media account. This identifier is essential for linking and troubleshooting social network-related issues.

Reconnecting to Facebook

If prompted to reconnect to Facebook, this typically ensures synchronization of game progress and access to social features. Follow the on-screen instructions to reconnect.

Consent Pop-ups

Consent pop-ups related to ad tracking appear to comply with privacy regulations. To manage or reset advertising identifiers, follow the steps provided in the pop-up, tailored for iOS or Android devices.

For detailed guidance and additional support, visit the Wizard of Oz Slots Help Center.
