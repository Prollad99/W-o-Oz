Understanding Billing and Purchases in Wizard of Oz Slots

Welcome to the world of Wizard of Oz Slots, a free-to-play mobile game developed by Zynga. In this article, we'll dive into the billing and purchase aspects of the game, providing you with a comprehensive guide to help you navigate the process seamlessly.

What is a "Free to Play" Game?

Wizard of Oz Slots is a "Free to Play" game, which means you can download and enjoy the entire game without paying a single penny. However, the game offers optional in-game purchases that allow you to enhance your gaming experience by acquiring virtual goods or unlocking additional features.

Billing Phone Support Hotline

If you encounter any billing-related issues or have questions regarding purchases, Zynga provides a dedicated Billing Phone Support Hotline. This hotline is available from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM, Monday to Sunday, Pacific Standard Time. You can access the support by navigating to the game settings, locating the "Help" or "Support" option, and selecting the "Contact" feature.

To reach the Billing Phone Support Hotline, you can call the following numbers based on your location:

  • North America: 1-800-762-2530
  • Europe: 0080093799642

Please note that the Billing Phone Support Hotline is available only in English, and it focuses specifically on billing-related queries.

Purchasing Virtual Goods

Wizard of Oz Slots allows you to purchase virtual goods using various payment methods, including major credit cards, PayPal, and payment processors such as Google Play, Apple iTunes, Facebook, Amazon, Zyngagames.com, and the Zynga Store.

It's important to note that the accepted payment methods may vary depending on the payment platform you're using and the game you're playing. For more information on acceptable payment methods for each platform, please refer to the respective help centers:

  • Apple (iOS) devices: Apple Help Center
  • Google (Android) devices: Google Play Help Center
  • Amazon: Amazon Help Center
  • Facebook: Facebook Help Center

Taxes and Fees

Depending on your location, taxes may be applied to your Zynga purchases. The price displayed on the purchase page will include any applicable taxes for your area. Taxes are applied based on the geographic location of your Internet-connected device.

For international purchases, applicable taxes or VAT will be included in the purchase price for certain countries, including Albania, Armenia, Australia, Belarus, the European Union, Iceland, India, Japan, New Zealand, Norway, Saudi Arabia, South Africa, Switzerland, Taiwan, and the United Arab Emirates.

In the United States, sales taxes are added to the purchase price in several states, including Arizona, Chicago, IL, Connecticut, D.C., Hawaii, Idaho, Kentucky, Massachusetts, Minnesota, New Mexico, New York, Pennsylvania, South Dakota, Tennessee, Texas, Utah, Vermont, Washington, Wisconsin, and other applicable states.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding taxes, please contact your state, province, or country's Tax Department.

Promotions and Sales

Zynga may occasionally offer limited-time promotions or sales within the game or on the add coin or cash page. These promotions and sales vary depending on the game, your gameplay, and will be available for a limited time only. Unfortunately, Zynga cannot issue refunds, credits, or extra points for purchases made before or after the promotion has expired.

Redeeming Gift Cards

If you have an Apple (iTunes) Gift Card, you can redeem it and use the credits in your game. Here's how:

For iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch:

  1. Open the App Store app.
  2. Tap "Today," then tap your photo in the upper-right corner.
  3. Tap "Redeem Gift Card or Code," then sign in with your Apple ID.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to redeem your gift card using the camera or enter the code manually.

For Mac or PC:

  1. Open iTunes and sign in with your Apple ID.
  2. From the menu bar, choose "Account" > "Redeem."
  3. Sign in with your Apple ID.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to redeem your gift card using the camera or enter the code manually.

Once redeemed, the store credit will be used for each purchase you make in any of the stores, including Apple Music subscriptions and iCloud storage, until it's gone.

Managing Subscriptions

Some Zynga games and apps offer subscriptions that allow you to set up monthly recurring payments. If you've set up a subscription, you can cancel it at any time by following these steps:

  1. Visit your payment settings.
  2. Under "Subscriptions," click "Deactivate" next to the subscription you want to cancel.

The cancellation of your subscription will become effective at the end of your current billing period, ensuring you continue to receive the subscription benefits until then.

Requesting Refunds

Refund requests should be submitted to the original payment processor (e.g., Apple for purchases made in-app on an iOS device), as each platform has its own refund policies and procedures. Zynga's Refund Policy can be found at https://www.take2games.com/refund-policy.

Here are the links to request a refund for different payment processors:

  • Apple Refund Request: Apple Support
  • Amazon Refund Request: Amazon Support
  • Facebook or Google Purchases: Contact Zynga's customer support by clicking the "Contact Us" button at the bottom of the page.
  • Zynga Store Refund Request: Zynga Billing Support

When requesting a refund, be sure to provide the following information to assist with the investigation:

  • The exact amount of the items purchased and the approximate time of purchase.
  • Your player ID/UID or ZID (located in the game settings).
  • A screenshot of your purchase receipt or the error you're receiving.

Additional Security Measures

To help prevent accidental purchases on your device, you can enable additional security features that require a password or authentication for every purchase. Here are the links to enable these features:

  • Apple (iOS) devices: Manage iTunes Store and App Store password preferences and Prevent in-app purchases from the App Store
  • Android (Google) devices: Require a password or authentication for purchases
  • Facebook account: Set up a PIN for Facebook Pay

Enabling these security measures can help prevent unauthorized or accidental purchases on your device.

By understanding the billing and purchase processes, you can make informed decisions and enjoy the game with confidence.
