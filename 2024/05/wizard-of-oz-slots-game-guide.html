Wizard of Oz Slots Game Guide

Wizard of Oz Slots is an engaging and feature-packed game that offers a variety of exciting events, bonuses, and rewards to keep players entertained and rewarded for their gameplay. This comprehensive guide will provide you with detailed information about the various aspects of the game, helping you navigate through the different features and maximize your enjoyment.

Events and Features

Enchanted Vaults

Enchanted Vaults is a thrilling feature that rewards you with additional prizes after achieving big wins in the game. Whenever you score a Mega Win or higher, you'll receive a beautiful vault containing extra prizes. There are four different types of vaults: common, uncommon, rare, and rainbow. The higher your bet, the better the chances of getting better prizes in the vault. You can store up to four vaults at a time, and if you receive a fifth vault, you'll need to decide whether to give up the new one or open an existing vault immediately.

Jackpot Days

Jackpot Days is a time-limited event that gives you the opportunity to enter a jackpot and win big when you purchase eligible credit packages on the Buy Page. After making an eligible purchase, you'll automatically be entered into a jackpot. The larger the credit package you buy, the higher your chances of winning the jackpot. If you don't win the jackpot, you'll receive a consolation prize.

Wicked Tower

Wicked Tower is a daily challenge where you climb the Tower of the Wicked Witch, choosing prizes on each floor. If you reach the top floor or decide to cash out early, you'll earn all the prizes you've picked. However, be cautious, as the Wicked Witch may catch you, causing you to lose your collected prizes. You can use Gems to continue playing if caught.

Trail of Wonder

Trail of Wonder is a multi-day event where your goal is to travel down the Trail of Wonder by collecting tokens earned from spinning on any machine. As you progress down the trail, you'll earn fabulous prizes. Bigger bets earn tokens faster, and completing the trail grants you additional rewards.

Rainbow Rush

Rainbow Rush is a real-time weekly competition where you compete against other players on your division leaderboard. Win credits from spinning on any machine to move up the leaderboard. Finish in the Promotion Zone to move up the divisions for better rewards, including larger Buy Bonuses and Hourly Bonuses. If you finish in the Drop Zone, you'll be demoted to a lower division.

Glinda's Jackpot Wheel

Glinda's Jackpot Wheel is a High Roller World event where you spin wheels with Glinda to win Jackpots and Multipliers. Fill up your Heart Meter by spinning in High Roller World machines to access the wheels. Landing on Jackpot wedges will take you to higher-tier wheels with bigger Multipliers. Collect Jackpots to complete your Rewards Card and win the Grand Prize.

Emerald City Empire

Emerald City Empire is a multi-day event where you build and expand the Emerald City from a small settlement. As you improve the city, the Credit Bank will accumulate more credits for you to collect periodically. You can upgrade buildings by spending Emeralds found while spinning on any machine and earn rewards for leveling up and expanding your city.

Prestige

Prestige is a rewards program that provides players with exclusive benefits for playing Wizard of Oz Slots. You can earn Prestige Points through credit purchases, spinning on machines, and completing challenges. Accumulating enough Prestige Points will grant you a Prestige Membership Pass, which unlocks benefits like Daily Credits Back on every bet, Free Gifts on credit purchases, and Daily Surprise Gifts.

Minigames

Most machines in Wizard of Oz Slots offer an On-Reel Picker Minigame, where you can pick a prize from three choices. Some machines also feature Unique Minigames, which are detailed in the Paytable and instructions for each machine.

Clubs

Clubs is a social feature that connects you with a group of other players. You can invite your Facebook friends to join your Club and collect rewards when your Clubmates win big. Club members can also send gifts to each other without counting towards the daily gift limit.

Trophies

The Trophies feature allows you to collect Trophies by completing various achievements. Visit your Profile to check the available Trophies and the tasks required to earn them. Each Trophy has points that contribute to increasing your Trophy ranking.

Bonus Buddy

Bonus Buddy is your little companion that provides amazing perks if you keep it active. Feed your Buddy banana treats to activate perks like Increased Hourly Bonus, Banana Respin (turning losing spins into winning ones), and Banana Bonus (credit rewards on spins). Leveling up your Buddy permanently boosts these perks.

Daily Challenges Deluxe

Daily Challenges Deluxe is a set of fun challenges you can complete to earn prizes. Complete Daily Challenges for smaller prizes and Deluxe Points, and Seasonal Challenges for larger prizes and more Deluxe Points. Accumulate Deluxe Points to progress on the Prizes meter and unlock milestones, including Elite Prizes if you purchase the Elite Pass.

Explore A Board

Explore A Board is a multi-day board game event where your goal is to make it to the center of the board by spinning a wheel and moving spaces. Reach the center to earn the Board Reward and move on to a new board with bigger prizes. You can earn additional rewards by landing on certain spaces, such as Credits Reward, Reward Up, and Mystery Reward spaces.

Horsey Bank

The Horsey Bank is your personal bank that fills up with credits when you spin on a machine or complete a purchase. Once the bank is full, you'll receive a special offer to claim all the credits inside. You can customize your Horsey's name by clicking on the Horsey icon above the Spin button or Collect Bonus button.

Other Features and Information

  • Gems are a currency used for various purposes, such as additional plays of Wicked Tower or keeping the Wicked Witch at bay. You can earn Gems slowly through certain activities or purchase them from the Gem Store.
  • Gift collection limits are based on your VIP tier, and gifts from Clubmates and Yellow Brick Road teammates are counted separately.
  • Your Coin Balance does not sync across multiple devices, allowing you to play with different coin totals on each device.
  • The Bigger Free Bonus reward earned when leveling up is added to your Daily Bonus, not your Coin Balance.
  • Win types include Big Win, Mega Win, Epic Win, Over the Rainbow Win, and 5 of a Kind.
  • Odds of winning the Top Prize amount for each Scratcher are provided.
  • The Auto-Spin feature allows you to spin continuously by holding the "Spin" button for 2 seconds.
  • Leveling up can increase your Max Bet amount, Daily Bonus amount, and provide Free Coins or Bonus Credits on purchases.
  • Friends on the progression path are represented by their profile pictures if their games are Facebook-connected.

With this comprehensive guide, you'll have a better understanding of the various events, features, and mechanics in Wizard of Oz Slots. Explore the game, participate in exciting events, and enjoy the rewards and bonuses as you embark on an enchanting journey through the wonderful world of Oz.
